  • Modern weather forecasting relies heavily on computer-based models (e.g., GFS, ECMWF). These models simulate atmospheric conditions based on current data and mathematical equations to predict future weather patterns.
  • Weather analysis often involves comparing current conditions with historical weather data. This helps in identifying trends, patterns, and deviations that can signal potential weather anomalies.
