Yellow Alert in Kerala

IMD has issued yellow alerts for several districts in Kerala, including Thiruvananthapuram and Kollam, anticipating isolated heavy rainfall. Residents in areas prone to landslides and flash floods are advised to evacuate to safer locations as rainfall amounts could reach between 64.5 mm and 115.5 m​

October Rainfall Forecast

The IMD predicts above-normal rainfall across southern, central, and northeastern India from October to December 2024. Southern regions, including Tamil Nadu and Kerala, are expected to experience significant rainfall, which could provide relief for agricultural activities. In contrast, some northwestern areas may see normal to below-normal rainfall during this period ​

Record Monsoon Rainfall

The 2024 monsoon season has broken a five-year record for very-heavy and extremely-heavy rainfall events in India. The India Meteorological Department (IMD) reported 2,632 instances of very-heavy rainfall and 473 instances of extremely-heavy rainfall. This unusual increase in extreme rain events has been attributed to climate change, highlighting its impact on India's weather patterns ​
